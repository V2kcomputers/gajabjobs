NTA UGC NET June 2026 – Course & Eligibility Details
Course Name Eligibility (Qualification)
NTA UGC NET June 2026 ✔ Candidates must have a Master’s Degree or equivalent with at least 55% marks (General/EWS).
✔ OBC-NCL / SC / ST / PwD / Third Gender candidates require minimum 50% marks.

✔ Final year / result awaited candidates are also eligible to apply.
(They must complete their Master’s Degree within 2 years with required marks).

✔ Candidates with a 4-Year Bachelor’s Degree (minimum 75% marks, 70% for reserved categories)
are eligible for JRF and Ph.D admission (not eligible for Assistant Professor).

✔ Ph.D holders (who completed PG before 1991) get 5% relaxation in marks.

✔ For more detailed eligibility conditions, candidates are advised to read the official notification carefully.
