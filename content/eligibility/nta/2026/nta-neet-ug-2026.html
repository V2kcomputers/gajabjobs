NEET (UG) 2026 Eligibility Criteria
Eligibility Parameter Details
Nationality Indian Nationals, NRIs, OCIs, PIOs, and Foreign Nationals are eligible
Minimum Age Candidate must have completed 17 years of age on or before 31 December 2026
Maximum Age No upper age limit
Educational Qualification 10+2 or equivalent examination with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English as core subjects
Minimum Marks Required General Category: 50%
OBC/SC/ST: 40%
PwBD (General): 45%
Open School / Private Candidates Eligible as per NMC regulations
Number of Attempts No restriction on the number of attempts
NEET Requirement Qualifying NEET (UG) is mandatory for admission to MBBS, BDS, AYUSH, BVSc & AH, and B.Sc Nursing (MNS)
Note: Candidates are advised to carefully read the official eligibility notification for detailed and updated information.
