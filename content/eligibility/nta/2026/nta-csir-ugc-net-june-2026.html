Joint CSIR-UGC NET June 2026 Eligibility Details
Exam Name Qualification / Eligibility
Joint CSIR-UGC NET June 2026 Candidates having M.Sc, Master’s Degree or equivalent qualification with minimum 55% marks (50% marks for OBC-NCL/SC/ST/PwD/Third Gender candidates) can apply. 4-Year Bachelor Degree holders with minimum 75% marks are also eligible. Candidates having B.E/B.Tech, B.Pharma, MBBS, Integrated BS-MS and Final Year/Result Awaited qualification can also apply.

Read the official notification carefully for detailed eligibility criteria.
