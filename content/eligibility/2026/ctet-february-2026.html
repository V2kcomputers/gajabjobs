CTET 2026 – Post Name and Minimum Qualification
Post Name Qualification
Primary Teacher (Class 1 to 5) – Paper I Minimum qualification as per NCTE norms such as:
• Senior Secondary + D.El.Ed / B.El.Ed
• Graduation + D.El.Ed
(Eligibility must follow NCTE guidelines)
Upper Primary Teacher (Class 6 to 8) – Paper II Minimum qualification as per NCTE norms such as:
• Graduation + B.Ed
• B.El.Ed / D.El.Ed + Graduation
(Eligibility must follow NCTE guidelines)
