HTET 2025 Qualification – Post Wise
Post Name Required Qualification
PRT (Level-1)
Class I to V		 Senior Secondary (10+2) with at least 50% marks and 2-Year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed)
OR
Senior Secondary (10+2) with at least 50% marks and 4-Year Bachelor of Elementary Education (B.El.Ed)
OR
Graduation with 2-Year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed).
Hindi or Sanskrit must be studied at Matric or Higher level.
TGT (Level-2)
Class VI to VIII		 Graduation with at least 50% marks and B.Ed
OR
Graduation with at least 45% marks and B.Ed (as per NCTE norms)
OR
Senior Secondary (10+2) with at least 50% marks and 4-Year Integrated B.A./B.Sc. + B.Ed.
Subject concerned must be studied in Graduation.
Hindi or Sanskrit at Matric or Higher level is compulsory.
PGT (Level-3)
Class IX to XII		 Post Graduation (PG) in concerned subject with at least 50% marks and B.Ed.
Hindi or Sanskrit at Matric or Higher level is mandatory.
