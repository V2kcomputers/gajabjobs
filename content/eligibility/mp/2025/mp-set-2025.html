MP SET 2025 – Exam/Post & Qualification
Exam / Post Name Qualification
Madhya Pradesh State Eligibility Test (MP SET 2025) Master’s Degree (Post Graduation) in the relevant subject from a UGC-recognized university with 55% marks (or 50% for SC/ST/OBC (Non-Creamy Layer)/EWS/PwD). Final-year/Result-awaited PG students are provisionally eligible.
