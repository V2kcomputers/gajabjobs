Aadhaar Card – Important Information
Details Description
Document Name Aadhaar Card
Issuing Authority UIDAI (Unique Identification Authority of India)
Aadhaar Number 12-digit unique identity number
Type of ID Proof of Identity and Proof of Address
Used For Bank account, SIM card, government schemes, pension, scholarship, subsidy, KYC, etc.
Eligibility Any resident of India, including children
Required Documents Proof of Identity, Proof of Address, Date of Birth document (as per UIDAI list)
How to Enrol Visit nearest Aadhaar Enrolment/Update Centre with required documents and biometrics
Download Aadhaar From UIDAI website or mAadhaar app using Aadhaar Number / Enrolment ID and OTP
PDF Password Format First 4 letters of name in CAPITAL + Year of Birth (e.g., VIKR2001)
Update Options Name, Address, Date of Birth, Mobile Number, Email, Photo, Biometrics (as per UIDAI rules)
Update Charges ₹50 for demographic update, ₹100 for biometric update (may change as per UIDAI)
Official Website https://uidai.gov.in and https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in
Important Note Aadhaar is an identity proof, not proof of citizenship.
