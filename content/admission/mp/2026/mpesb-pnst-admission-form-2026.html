PNST 2026 B.Sc. Nursing Admission – Eligibility Details
Course Name Eligibility
PNST (B.Sc. Nursing)
  • Must be an Indian Citizen.
  • Must have passed Class 12th (10+2) with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) subjects.
  • General Category candidates must have secured a minimum of 45% aggregate marks.
  • SC/ST/OBC candidates must have secured a minimum of 40% aggregate marks.
  • Passing English as a subject is mandatory.
  • Candidate must be at least 17 years of age as on 31 December 2026.
  • Candidate should be medically fit.
  • For detailed eligibility criteria and other admission requirements, candidates are advised to read the official notification carefully.
