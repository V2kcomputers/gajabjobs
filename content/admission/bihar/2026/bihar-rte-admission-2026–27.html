Eligibility Details for RTE 25% Admission 2026–27
Category Eligibility Details
Minority Group Children belonging to SC, ST, OBC (including reserved categories) and Minority communities whose parents/guardians have an annual income of up to ₹1,00,000.
Economically Weaker Section (EWS) Children from all castes/communities whose parents/guardians have an annual income of less than ₹2,00,000.
Orphan Children Children who do not have parents.
Age Limit The child must be 6 years or older as on 01 April 2026.
Eligible Date of Birth: 02 April 2018 to 01 April 2020.
