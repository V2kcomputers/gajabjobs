Post Name / Service Minimum Educational Qualification
Indian Administrative Service (IAS) Graduate degree from a University incorporated by an Act of Central/State Legislature or other educational institution established by an Act of Parliament or declared to be deemed as a University under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956, or equivalent qualification.
Indian Police Service (IPS) Graduate degree or equivalent qualification.
Indian Foreign Service (IFS – Civil) Graduate degree or equivalent qualification.
Indian Forest Service (IFoS) Bachelor’s Degree with at least one of the subjects: Animal Husbandry & Veterinary Science, Botany, Chemistry, Geology, Mathematics, Physics, Statistics or Zoology OR Bachelor’s Degree in Agriculture or equivalent qualification.
Indian Revenue Service (Income Tax) Graduate degree or equivalent qualification.
Indian Revenue Service (Customs & Indirect Taxes) Graduate degree or equivalent qualification.
Indian Audit & Accounts Service (IA&AS) Graduate degree or equivalent qualification.
Indian Railway Management Service (IRMS) Graduate degree or equivalent qualification.
Other Central Services (Group ‘A’ & ‘B’) Graduate degree or equivalent qualification.
