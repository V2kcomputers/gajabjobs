We respect the intellectual property rights of others and try to give proper credit or references where applicable. If any content appears to be reproduced from external sources, it is either:

You are not allowed to:

4. DMCA Takedown Requests

If you are a copyright owner, or authorized to act on behalf of a copyright owner, and believe that any content on GajabJobs.com infringes your copyright, you may send us a DMCA complaint / takedown request.

Please include the following details in your email:

  • Your full name and contact details (email address is mandatory).
  • A clear description of the copyrighted work that you claim has been infringed.
  • The exact URL(s) on GajabJobs.com where the allegedly infringing content is located.
  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  • A statement that the information in your notice is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf.

6. Our Response to DMCA Notices

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, we may:

  • Remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing content
  • Review the material and contact the content contributor (if any)
  • Take further steps as required under applicable law

7. No False Claims

Submitting false or misleading DMCA complaints can lead to legal consequences. Please ensure that you are the lawful owner of the content or have the authority to act on behalf of the owner before sending a takedown request.

We take copyright claims seriously and aim to resolve genuine issues swiftly. Our goal is to maintain a fair balance between providing useful public information and respecting intellectual property rights.
