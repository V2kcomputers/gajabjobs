Bihar BPSC AES Notification 2025 : How To Apply
  • Visit the Official Website: Go to the official BTSC website btsc.bihar.gov.in
  • Read the Official Notification: Carefully read the complete advertisement/notification for eligibility and instructions.
  • Register as a New User: If you are a new user, click on "New Registration" and fill in the basic details to create your account.
  • Login to Your Account: Use your registered email/mobile number and password to log in.
  • Fill the Application Form: Enter personal details, qualifications, and other required information.
  • Upload Documents: Upload scanned copies of your photograph, signature, and relevant documents.
  • Pay the Application Fee: Pay the fee online via Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking / UPI.
  • Preview and Submit: Check all details carefully and submit the form.
  • Print the Final Application Form: After successful submission, download or print the final application form for your records.
