How To Apply for Apprentice Vacancy 2025 :
  • Go to : ongcapprentices.ongc.co.in (official ONGC Apprentice portal)
  • Choose portal as per qualification : For ITI Trades (Sl.No 1‚Äì29) - apprenticeshipindia.gov.in & For Graduate / Diploma Trades (Sl.No 30‚Äì39) - nats.education.gov.in
  • Register yourself : Create login ID and complete your profile on respective portal
  • Open application link : Select the work centre and trade of your choice
  • Fill details carefully : Name, Date of Birth, Qualification, Mobile Number, Email ID etc
