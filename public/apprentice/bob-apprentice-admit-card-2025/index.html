How To Apply for BOB Apprentice Vacancy 2025 :
  • Register First : Visit NATS Portal nats.education.gov.in. and complete your registration.
  • Login and Search : Log in to your account and search for Bank of Baroda apprenticeship opportunity.
  • Apply Online : Click on the advertisement and then select Apply for this Opportunity.
  • Enter Details : Fill in all required information such as name, email, mobile number, category, and preferred state.
  • Upload Documents : Upload scanned copies of your photo, signature, 10th, 12th, and graduation mark sheets.
  • Pay Application Fee : Visit the BFSI SSC portal and make the payment through the online mode only.
  • Confirm Submission : After successful payment, check your email for the Application cum Examination Form.
  • Note IDs : Write down your Enrollment ID / Apprentice Registration Code for future reference.
  • Attend Exams : Appear for the Online Exam, Document Verification, and Local Language Test.
  • Accept Contract : Once selected, accept your Apprenticeship Contract on NATS/NAPS portal and start training.
