Uttar Pradesh Teacher Eligibility Test (UPTET) admission 2026 : Short Details
UPTET 2026 Eligibility Criteria (Primary & Upper Primary Level)
Level Minimum Qualification Required Training / Degree
Primary Level (Class 1–5) Graduation (Bachelor Degree) Any one of the following:
• 2-Year D.El.Ed / BTC (Final Year or Passed)
• Distance BTC / Shiksha Mitra Training
• Diploma in Elementary Education (NCTE Approved)
• Diploma in Special Education (D.Ed Special)
• 4-Year B.El.Ed Course
• Urdu BTC Training
• Teaching Diploma (e.g., AMU)
• Moallim-e-Urdu Degree

For detailed eligibility criteria, please read the official notification carefully.
Upper Primary Level (Class 6–8) Graduation (Bachelor Degree) Any one of the following:
• 2-Year D.El.Ed / BTC
• B.Ed (50% Marks) – Final Year or Passed
• B.Ed (45% Marks as per NCTE Rules)
• B.Ed Special Education
• 4-Year BA/BSc + B.Ed Integrated Course
• B.Sc.Ed / BA.Ed (4-Year Course)
• B.El.Ed (4-Year Course)
• 1-Year B.Ed Special Education

For detailed eligibility criteria, please read the official notification carefully.
How To Apply for Uttar Pradesh Teacher Eligibility Test (UPTET) 2026 :
  • Visit Official Website : Go to the official UPTET website
  • Complete OTR Registration : Register using your mobile number and email ID
  • Login : Login using your Registration ID and Password
  • Fill Application Form : Enter your personal, educational and contact details carefully
  • Upload Documents : Upload your photo, signature and required documents
  • Preview Form : Review all entered details before final submission
  • Pay Application Fee : Pay the application fee through online mode
  • Final Submit : Submit the application form and take a printout for future use
