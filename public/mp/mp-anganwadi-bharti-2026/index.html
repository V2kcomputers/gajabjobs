How To Apply for Madhya Pradesh Anganwadi Worker & Helper Bharti Phase 1 Vacancy 2026 :
  • Visit Application Portal: Open the official MP Online Selection Portal in your web browser.
  • Read Notification: Carefully read the MP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 notification before applying.
  • Create Account: Complete the online registration using your mobile number and email ID.
  • Login Account: Sign in with your registered credentials to access the application form.
  • Choose Vacancy: Select the Anganwadi Worker or Anganwadi Assistant post as applicable.
  • Enter Details: Fill in your personal, educational, and address information accurately.
  • Select Location: Choose the eligible Gram Panchayat or Urban Ward for the advertised vacancy.
  • Upload Files: Upload your photograph, signature, and required documents in the prescribed format.
  • Review Form: Verify all entered details and uploaded documents before final submission.
  • Pay Fee: Pay the prescribed application fee through the available online payment methods.
  • Final Submit: Submit the online application form after successful fee payment.
  • Save Application: Download and keep a printout of the submitted application form for future reference.
