IBPS PO / MT Vacancy 2026
The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) has released the CRP PO/MT-XVI Recruitment 2026 Notification for the recruitment of 6,715 Probationary Officer (PO) / Management Trainee (MT) posts in participating public sector banks. Eligible graduates can submit their online applications from 01 July 2026 to 21 July 2026 through the official IBPS website. Candidates are advised to read the official notification carefully before applying to ensure they meet all eligibility conditions and complete the application process within the prescribed dates.

SBI Circle Based Officer CBO Result 2026
State Bank of India has released the SBI CBO Recruitment 2026 notification for 2050 Circle Based Officer posts. Online applications are invited from 29 January to 18 February 2026. Applicants must be graduates with a minimum of two years’ experience as an Officer in a Scheduled Commercial Bank or RRB. The recruitment process includes an online exam, experience screening, interview and local language test. Selected candidates will be appointed in the same circle applied for.

HTET Admit Card 2026
HTET 2025 is conducted by the Board of School Education Haryana to determine the eligibility of candidates for Primary Teacher (PRT), Trained Graduate Teacher (TGT), and Post Graduate Teacher (PGT) posts in Haryana. Candidates can apply for Level-1, Level-2, or Level-3 based on their educational qualification. The HTET certificate is valid for a lifetime, there is no limit on attempts, and the examination is conducted in offline (pen and paper) mode.

MP Anganwadi Bharti 2026
The Women and Child Development Department, Madhya Pradesh has invited online applications for MP Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 to fill 2,548 vacancies, including 781 Anganwadi Worker and 1,767 Anganwadi Assistant posts. Female candidates who have passed 12th and belong to the same Gram Panchayat (rural) or the same Ward (urban) where the vacancy is available are eligible to apply. The online application process starts on 01 July 2026 and closes on 13 July 2026, while the correction facility will remain available until 15 July 2026. Applicants must be between 18 and 35 years of age as on 01 January 2026 and are required to pay an application fee of ₹100 plus 18% GST through the MP Online Selection Portal.
