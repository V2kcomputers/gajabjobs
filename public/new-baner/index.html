UP Police CO Recruitment 2024 - Syllabus
UP Police invited online applications for the recruitment of Programmer Grade-2 and Computer Operator Grade-A from 07/01/2024 to 28/01/2024. The written examination is scheduled for 01/11/2025, and the admit card will be available three days before the exam. Eligible candidates—12th PCM passed with O Level or a related Diploma—can apply through the official UPPBPB website.
