UP Police SI Confidential, ASI Clerk and Accounts Online Form 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has released the notification for Police Sub Inspector (Confidential) and Police Assistant Sub Inspector (Clerk & Accounts) Recruitment 2025 under Advertisement No. PRPB-B (Cl.)/2025. A total of 537 vacancies are available. The online application process will start on 20/12/2025 and end on 19/01/2026. Candidates are required to meet the prescribed educational qualifications and age criteria as per the official notification. Selection will be conducted through a written examination followed by skill test (where applicable), document verification and medical examination. Only Physical Standard Test (PST) will be conducted; no PET is applicable.

MPPSC SET Exam 2025
The MP State Eligibility Test (MP SET 2025) is a state-level examination conducted by the Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission for determining eligibility for Assistant Professor recruitment in colleges and universities of Madhya Pradesh. The online application process will begin on 25 October 2025 and close on 20 November 2025. The written examination is scheduled to be held on 11 January 2026. Candidates are advised to review eligibility conditions, syllabus and subject list as per the official notification before submitting the application form.

SSC GD Constable Online Form 2026
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.

UPPSC Polytechnic Lecturer Online Form 2025
UPPSC has released the Technical Education (Teaching) Service Recruitment 2025 for 513 posts of Polytechnic Lecturers, Workshop Superintendent, and Architecture faculty. Eligible candidates can apply online from 02 December 2025 to 02 January 2026. Selection will be based on a written exam and interview. Engineering posts require First Class B.Tech/M.Tech, while non-engineering posts require First Class Master’s degree.
