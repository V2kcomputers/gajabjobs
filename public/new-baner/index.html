BPSC Sports Officer Exam Date Out 2025
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has announced 33 vacancies for the posts of District Sports Officer, Assistant Director (Sports/Youth), and Lecturer (SH & Colleges) under the Bihar Sports Service Cadre through Advt. No. 90/2025. Candidates with a Graduate Degree and a Diploma/PG Diploma in Sports Coaching from a recognized institute are eligible to apply. The online application window will remain open from 02 September 2025 to 30 September 2025, and applicants need to pay a fee of ₹100/- plus bank charges. This is a good opportunity for candidates with sports coaching qualifications to build their career in the Bihar Sports Department.

Bihar BPSC AEDO Online Form 2025 - Reopen
Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released the official notification for 935 Assistant Education Development Officer (AEDO) vacancies under the Education Department. The online application process will begin on 27 August 2025 and continue till 26 September 2025. Candidates with a graduate degree from a recognized university are eligible to apply. The salary will be as per Level-5 pay scale (₹29,200 + allowances). Applicants must pay an examination fee of ₹100/- plus bank charges through online mode. Interested candidates should visit the official website bpsc.bihar.gov.in to apply online and check detailed guidelines.

KVS NVS Teaching & Non-Teaching Online Form 2025
KVS NVS Recruitment 2025 offers more than 14967 Teaching and Non-Teaching vacancies under Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan and Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti. Eligible candidates can apply online from 14/11/2025 to 04/12/2025 for posts such as PRT, TGT, PGT, Principal, Clerk, Stenographer, Librarian and others. The application fee is ₹1500 for General, OBC and EWS categories, while SC, ST, PwD and all female candidates are exempted from fees. Qualifications vary by post and include 10th, 12th, Graduation, Post Graduation, B.Ed, D.El.Ed and CTET. The selection process includes a computer-based test followed by document verification.
