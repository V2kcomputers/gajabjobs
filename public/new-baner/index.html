CTET February 2026 Registration
The CTET February 2026 notification has been released, and the online application process is open from 27/11/2025 to 18/12/2025. Candidates can apply for Paper I (Class 1–5) and Paper II (Class 6–8) based on NCTE eligibility norms. The exam will be conducted on 08 February 2026 in offline OMR mode across multiple exam centers. The application fee is ₹1000 for General/OBC (single paper) and ₹500 for SC/ST/PwD candidates. The CTET certificate is valid for a lifetime, and all important details regarding syllabus, exam pattern, eligibility, and fee structure are provided in the official notification.

RRB NTPC Inter Level Online Form 2025
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released the notification for RRB NTPC Inter Level Recruitment 2025 (CEN 07/2025) with 3050 vacancies under the 10+2 (Undergraduate) category. Eligible candidates who have passed Intermediate (Class 12th) can apply online for posts like Junior Clerk cum Typist, Accounts Clerk cum Typist, Train Clerk, and Commercial cum Ticket Clerk. The application process will start from 28 October 2025 and continue till 27 November 2025. The selection process includes CBT-1, CBT-2, Typing Skill Test (for Clerk/Typist posts), Document Verification, and Medical Examination. The pay scale ranges from ₹19,900 to ₹21,700 (Level-2 & Level-3 as per 7th CPC) with additional allowances. Candidates are advised to check the official notification for complete eligibility, age limit, application fees, and exam details.

RRB NTPC Graduate Level Online Form 2025
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has announced the RRB NTPC Graduate Level Recruitment 2025 (CEN 06/2025) with around 5800 vacancies under the graduate category. Eligible candidates with a Bachelor’s degree can apply online for posts such as Station Master, Goods Guard, Senior Clerk cum Typist, Junior Accounts Assistant, Commercial Apprentice, and Traffic Assistant. The online application window will remain open from 21 October 2025 to 20 November 2025. The selection process includes CBT-1, CBT-2, Computer Based Aptitude Test (for Station Master & Traffic Assistant), Typing Skill Test (for Clerk/Typist posts), Document Verification, and Medical Examination. The salary ranges from ₹25,500 to ₹35,400 (Level-4 to Level-6 as per 7th CPC) along with allowances. Candidates must check the official notification for detailed eligibility, post-wise vacancies, exam pattern, and other important updates.

EMRS Teaching & Non-Teaching Exam Date 2025
The National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS) has announced the EMRS Recruitment 2025 (ESSE-2025) notification for a total of 7267 vacancies across teaching and non-teaching posts. The vacancies include Principal, PGT, TGT, Female Staff Nurse, Hostel Warden, Accountant, Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA), and Lab Attendant. Candidates can apply online until 23rd October 2025, with application fees ranging from ₹500 to ₹2500 depending on the post and category. The eligibility criteria vary by position, requiring qualifications from 10th/12th to Graduate, Postgraduate, B.Ed., CTET, or professional degrees. The age limit is set between 30 to 50 years, with applicable relaxations for reserved categories. This recruitment provides a major opportunity for aspirants seeking teaching and non-teaching jobs in Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) across India.
