Indian Army SSC Tech 67th Women Online Form 2026
Indian Army has released the SSC (Tech) 67 Women Recruitment 2026 for unmarried female engineering graduates. A total of 29 posts are available across Civil, Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics, and Mechanical branches. Online applications are open from 2026-01-06 to 2026-02-04 on the official website joinindianarmy.nic.in. Selection will be based on shortlisting, SSB interview, medical examination, and merit list. Training duration is 49 weeks and candidates will receive a stipend of ₹56,100 per month during training

Indian Army SSC Technical 67 Men Online Form 2026
Indian Army has released the SSC (Technical) 67 Men Recruitment 2026 for 350 Technical Officer posts. Unmarried male engineering graduates aged 20 to 27 years can apply online from 07/01/2026 to 05/02/2026 through the official website. Selection will be done through shortlisting, SSB interview, medical examination and merit list. Selected candidates will undergo 49 weeks of training at OTA Gaya and will be commissioned as Lieutenant with an attractive salary and allowances.

RRB Ministerial & Isolated Category Online Form 2025
RRB Ministerial & Isolated Category Recruitment 2025 notification has been released under CEN No. 08/2025 for a total of 311 vacancies across various posts including Junior Translator, Staff & Welfare Inspector, Publicity Inspector, Law Assistant and others. The recruitment is conducted by the Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) under the Ministry of Railways, Government of India. Eligible male and female candidates can apply online from 30/12/2025 to 29/01/2026. Selection will be based on Computer Based Test (CBT), Document Verification and Medical Examination as per RRB rules.

RRB Group D Online Form 2026
RRB Level-1 / Group D Recruitment 2025 has been announced through CEN No. 09/2025 by the Railway Recruitment Boards for approximately 22000 vacancies across all RRB zones. The recruitment is for Level-1 posts under the 7th CPC Pay Matrix with an initial salary of ₹18000 per month. The online application process will be conducted in 2026 from 21 January to 20 February 2026 through the official RRB website. Candidates aged between 18 and 33 years as on 01 January 2026 will be eligible to apply, subject to relaxation as per rules. Detailed post-wise eligibility qualification selection process and syllabus will be released in the full notification on official RRB websites.

MPESB Group 1 Subgroup 2 Online Form 2025
MPESB Group 1 Subgroup 2 Recruitment 2025 has been announced for a total of 474 various posts under different departments of the Government of Madhya Pradesh.
