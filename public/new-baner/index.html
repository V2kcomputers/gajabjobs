Union Bank of India Apprentice Online Form 2026
Union Bank of India Apprentice Recruitment 2026 has been released for 1865 vacancies. The online application started on 29 April 2026 and the last date to apply is 19 May 2026. Candidates with a graduation degree in any discipline and age between 20 to 28 years are eligible to apply. The selection process includes an online exam and local language test. Selected candidates will receive a stipend of ₹15,000 to ₹20,000 per month during the 1-year apprenticeship training. Candidates are advised to apply online before the last date and read the official notification carefully for complete details.
