NTA NCHM JEE 2026 Admission Test
NCHM JEE 2026 is a national level entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to the B.Sc. Hospitality & Hotel Administration (B.Sc. HHA) course. Candidates who have passed or are appearing in 10+2 with English as a subject are eligible to apply. The examination is conducted in Computer Based Test (CBT) mode, and admission to Central, State, and Private Institutes of Hotel Management is offered based on rank, counselling, and seat availability.

NTA UGC NET December 2025 - Admit Card
The UGC NET December 2025 Notification has been released by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for determining the eligibility of candidates for Assistant Professor and Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) in Indian universities and colleges. Candidates with a Master’s degree (55% marks for General and 50% for reserved categories) can apply online from 07 October to 07 November 2025 at ugcnet.nta.nic.in. The exam will be conducted in Computer Based Test (CBT) mode across multiple subjects.

RRB Group D Online Form 2026
RRB Level-1 / Group D Recruitment 2025 has been announced through CEN No. 09/2025 by the Railway Recruitment Boards for approximately 22000 vacancies across all RRB zones. The recruitment is for Level-1 posts under the 7th CPC Pay Matrix with an initial salary of ₹18000 per month. The online application process will be conducted in 2026 from 21 January to 20 February 2026 through the official RRB website. Candidates aged between 18 and 33 years as on 01 January 2026 will be eligible to apply, subject to relaxation as per rules. Detailed post-wise eligibility qualification selection process and syllabus will be released in the full notification on official RRB websites.
