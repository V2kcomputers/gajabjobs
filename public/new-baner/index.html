Super 100 Examination Admit Card 2026
Super 100 is a state-level admission examination conducted for meritorious students studying in government schools of Madhya Pradesh and is administered by the Madhya Pradesh State Open School. Through this examination, students are granted admission into Class 11 residential schools, where selected candidates receive free coaching for competitive examinations like JEE, NEET, and CLAT, along with free accommodation, meals, and study materials. This scheme has been launched to provide quality education and better career opportunities to economically weaker but talented students. Selection is based on the entrance examination and merit, ensuring equal opportunities for deserving students.
