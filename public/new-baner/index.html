NTA NEET UG Admit Card 2026
NEET (UG) 2026 is a national-level entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate medical courses across India. It is mandatory for admission to MBBS, BDS, AYUSH, BVSc & AH, and B.Sc Nursing (MNS) courses. Candidates must be 17 years old by 31 December 2026 and must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English. There is no upper age limit or attempt restriction. The examination will be conducted offline on 03 May 2026 for a duration of three hours in 13 languages.

CRPF Constable Tradesman Online Form 2026
CRPF Constable (Technical & Tradesmen & Pioneer) Recruitment 2026 notification has been issued for 9,195 vacancies. Out of these, 9,175 posts are for Technical & Tradesmen (Male: 9,096, Female: 79) and 20 posts are for Pioneer Wing (Mason and Electrician). SSC GD Constable Exam Admit Card 2026
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.
