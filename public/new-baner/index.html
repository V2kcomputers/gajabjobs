EMRS Teaching & Non-Teaching Exam Date 2025
The National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS) has announced the EMRS Recruitment 2025 (ESSE-2025) notification for a total of 7267 vacancies across teaching and non-teaching posts. The vacancies include Principal, PGT, TGT, Female Staff Nurse, Hostel Warden, Accountant, Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA), and Lab Attendant. Candidates can apply online until 23rd October 2025, with application fees ranging from ₹500 to ₹2500 depending on the post and category. The eligibility criteria vary by position, requiring qualifications from 10th/12th to Graduate, Postgraduate, B.Ed., CTET, or professional degrees. The age limit is set between 30 to 50 years, with applicable relaxations for reserved categories. This recruitment provides a major opportunity for aspirants seeking teaching and non-teaching jobs in Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) across India.

The Intelligence Bureau (IB), under the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), has announced the recruitment of 3717 posts for Assistant Central Intelligence Officer (ACIO) Grade-II / Executive. Application | Dates | Requirements | Important Links
Uttar Pradesh Home Guard Online Form 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Home Guard Recruitment 2025 has announced 41,424 vacancies for Home Guard (Male/Female) posts across all districts of the state. The online application process will run from 18 November 2025 to 17 December 2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10 (High School) from a recognized board and must be between 18 to 30 years of age as on 01 July 2025. The selection process includes Physical Standards Test (PST) and Physical Efficiency Test (PET), where male candidates must complete 4.8 km in 28 minutes and female candidates must complete 2.4 km in 16 minutes. The application fee is ₹400 for General/OBC/EWS and ₹300 for SC/ST candidates. The complete district-wise vacancy list and PET/PST rules are provided in the official notification.
