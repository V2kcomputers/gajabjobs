RRB Paramedical Staff Application Status 2025
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB), Ministry of Railways, Government of India, has released the Centralized Employment Notice (CEN No. 03/2025) for Paramedical Categories Recruitment 2025 with a total of 434 vacancies across various posts including Nursing Superintendent, Pharmacist, Dialysis Technician, ECG Technician, Radiographer, and Lab Assistant. The online application process will begin on 09 August 2025 and will remain open until 08 September 2025 (11:59 PM). The educational qualifications vary by post, ranging from 10+2 with Diploma/Certificate to B.Sc. in relevant fields, and the age limit is 18 to 40 years as on 01 January 2026. The selected candidates will receive a salary ranging between ₹21,700 and ₹44,900 as per the 7th CPC Pay Matrix (Level 2 to Level 7). The selection process will consist of a Computer Based Test (CBT), Document Verification, and a Medical Examination. Candidates can apply through the official RRB regional websites such as rrbahmedabad.gov.in and others.

Railway RRB Technician Application Status 2025
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released the RRB Technician Recruitment 2025 notification for Technician Grade-1 (Signal) and Technician Grade-3 (Open Line) posts. Around 6238 vacancies will be filled. Grade-1 requires a B.Sc, BE/B.Tech or 3-year diploma in Physics, Electronics, Computer Science, IT, or Instrumentation. Grade-3 requires 10th pass with ITI in relevant trade from NCVT/SCVT. Eligible candidates can apply online. Please read the official notification for full details.
