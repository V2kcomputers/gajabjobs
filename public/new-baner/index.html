CCRH Research Officer & Other Posts Admit Card 2025
The Central Council for Research in Homoeopathy (CCRH), under the Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India, has released the CCRH Recruitment 2025 Notification (Advt. Interested applicants should read the full notification for eligibility, qualification, age limit, and application fee details before applying.

IAF AFCAT 01/2026 Batch Online Form 2025
The Indian Air Force (IAF) has announced the AFCAT 01/2026 Batch Recruitment 2025 for Flying and Ground Duty (Technical & Non-Technical) branches. The online application process will begin on 10 November 2025 and continue till 9 December 2025 through the official website afcat.cdac.in. Candidates holding a graduate or engineering degree with Physics and Mathematics at 10+2 level can apply. The selection process includes an online written test, AFSB interview, and medical examination. Successful candidates will join the AFCAT 01/2026 course commencing in January 2026 as Group-A Gazetted Officers in the Indian Air Force
