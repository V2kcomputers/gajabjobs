CTET February 2026 - Admit Card
The CTET February 2026 notification has been released, and the online application process is open from 27/11/2025 to 18/12/2025. Candidates can apply for Paper I (Class 1–5) and Paper II (Class 6–8) based on NCTE eligibility norms. The exam will be conducted on 08 February 2026 in offline OMR mode across multiple exam centers. The application fee is ₹1000 for General/OBC (single paper) and ₹500 for SC/ST/PwD candidates. The CTET certificate is valid for a lifetime, and all important details regarding syllabus, exam pattern, eligibility, and fee structure are provided in the official notification.

NTA UGC NET December 2025 - Result
The UGC NET December 2025 Notification has been released by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for determining the eligibility of candidates for Assistant Professor and Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) in Indian universities and colleges.
