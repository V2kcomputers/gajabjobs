UPSC CAPF Assistant Commandant Vacancy 2026
The CAPF Assistant Commandant 2026 Notification has been officially released for a total of 349 vacancies in BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, and SSB. The online application process started on 20/02/2026 and the last date to apply is 12/03/2026 (till 6:00 PM). The written examination will be conducted on 19/07/2026. Candidates must hold a Bachelor’s Degree from a recognized university. The age limit is 20 to 25 years as on 01 August 2026, with age relaxation applicable as per government rules for reserved categories. The selection process includes Written Examination, Physical Standards Test (PST), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Interview/Personality Test, and Medical Examination. Both male and female candidates are eligible to apply.
