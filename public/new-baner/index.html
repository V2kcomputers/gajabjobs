DSSSB JE, AE, ASO Vacancy 2026
Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has released Advertisement No. 01/2026 for a total of 911 vacancies under Combined Examination 2026 for the posts of Legal Assistant, Assistant Engineer (Civil), Junior Engineer (Civil), and Grade-II (GNCTDSS) / Assistant Section Officer in various departments of the Government of NCT of Delhi. The online application process will start from 24/02/2026 and the last date to apply is 25/03/2026 through the official online portal. The required qualification varies post-wise such as Law Degree for Legal Assistant, Civil Engineering Degree/Diploma for AE and JE, and Bachelor’s Degree for ASO, with the age limit generally up to 30 years and relaxation applicable as per government rules.

RBI Assistant Online Form 2026
Reserve Bank of India has invited online applications for 650 Assistant posts under RBI Assistant Recruitment 2026. Candidates holding a Bachelor’s degree with minimum 50% marks (pass class for SC/ST/PwBD) and basic computer knowledge can apply from 16/02/2026 to 08/03/2026. The recruitment process consists of Preliminary Exam, Main Exam and Language Proficiency Test. The Prelims will be held on 11/04/2026 and the Mains on 07/06/2026. Selected candidates will receive a starting basic pay of ₹29,000 and an initial gross salary of around ₹58,514 per month.

RRB NTPC Graduate Level Vacancy 2025 - Exam Date
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has announced the RRB NTPC Graduate Level Recruitment 2025 (CEN 06/2025) with around 5800 vacancies under the graduate category. Eligible candidates with a Bachelor’s degree can apply online for posts such as Station Master, Goods Guard, Senior Clerk cum Typist, Junior Accounts Assistant, Commercial Apprentice, and Traffic Assistant. The online application window will remain open from 21 October 2025 to 20 November 2025. The selection process includes CBT-1, CBT-2, Computer Based Aptitude Test (for Station Master & Traffic Assistant), Typing Skill Test (for Clerk/Typist posts), Document Verification, and Medical Examination. The salary ranges from ₹25,500 to ₹35,400 (Level-4 to Level-6 as per 7th CPC) along with allowances. Candidates must check the official notification for detailed eligibility, post-wise vacancies, exam pattern, and other important updates.
