Indian Air Force Agniveervayu Intake 02/2027 Online Form
The Indian Air Force (IAF) has invited online applications for Agniveervayu Intake 02/2027 under the Agnipath Scheme. Unmarried male and female candidates who have passed 12th, Diploma, or Vocational courses can apply from 06 July 2026 to 26 July 2026. The recruitment process includes an Online Test, Physical Fitness Test (PFT), Adaptability Tests, Document Verification, and Medical Examination. Selected candidates will be enrolled for four years and will receive salary, allowances, insurance coverage, and Seva Nidhi benefits as per Agnipath Scheme rules.

IAF Airmen Group Y Medical Assistant Intake 02/2027 Online Form
The Indian Air Force (IAF) invites online applications for Airmen Group 'Y' Medical Assistant Intake 02/2027. Male Indian and Gorkha candidates meeting the required educational qualifications can submit their applications from 06 July 2026 to 26 July 2026. Eligible applicants must have completed 10+2 with PCB and English or hold a Diploma/B.Pharm/B.Sc in Pharmacy. The recruitment process consists of an Online Examination, Physical Fitness Test, Adaptability Tests, and Medical Examination. During training, selected candidates will receive a monthly stipend of ₹14,600, followed by a starting salary of ₹26,900 per month after training. The online test is scheduled for 22-23 September 2026.

UPSC CAPF Assistant Commandant Exam Date 2026
UPSC has released the CAPF Assistant Commandant 2026 notification for 349 posts across BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP and SSB. The application window is open from 20/02/2026 to 12/03/2026 (6:00 PM).
