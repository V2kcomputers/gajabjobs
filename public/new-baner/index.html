RRB Exam Calendar 2026
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released the official RRB Exam Calendar 2026 outlining the recruitment planning schedule for various railway posts. The calendar includes major categories such as Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP), Technicians, NTPC (Graduate and Under Graduate), Junior Engineer (JE), Paramedical, Ministerial & Isolated posts, Section Controller, and Level-1 (Group D). This calendar helps candidates understand the expected recruitment cycle; however, actual notifications and exam dates will be released separately through individual CENs by the respective RRBs.

SSC CPO SI 2025 - Answer Key
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for CPO SI Recruitment 2025, offering 3073 vacancies for Sub-Inspector posts in Delhi Police and CAPFs. Eligible candidates with a bachelor’s degree can apply online from 26 September to 16 October 2025. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Test (Paper-I & II), Physical Tests (PST/PET), Medical Examination, and Document Verification. This recruitment provides an excellent opportunity for graduates to join Delhi Police and CAPFs with a pay scale of Level-6 (₹35,400 – ₹1,12,400).

Bank of India Apprentice Online Form 2025
Bank of India Apprentice Recruitment 2025 has been announced for a total of 400 apprenticeship positions across various states of India. The recruitment is open to graduate candidates who meet the prescribed age and eligibility criteria. The online application process will be conducted from 25 December 2025 to 10 January 2026. Selection will be based on an online written test followed by a local language proficiency test. The apprenticeship training duration is one year, during which selected candidates will receive a monthly stipend of ₹13,000. This program is designed to provide hands-on banking experience but does not guarantee permanent employment in the bank.

MPESB Group 1 Subgroup 2 Online Form 2025
MPESB Group 1 Subgroup 2 Recruitment 2025 has been announced for a total of 474 various posts under different departments of the Government of Madhya Pradesh. The selection will be conducted through a written examination followed by further procedures as mentioned in the official notification.

PNB Local Bank Officer LBO 2025 - Exam Date
Punjab National Bank (PNB) has released the official notification for LBO Recruitment 2025, offering 750 Local Bank Officer (LBO) vacancies across India. Eligible graduates with at least one year of banking experience can apply online from 03/11/2025 to 23/11/2025 through the official website pnbindia.in. The selection process includes an online written test and interview, and the appointment will be made in the Junior Management Grade Scale-I. Candidates are advised to check their eligibility, fees, age limit, and local language proficiency before applying
