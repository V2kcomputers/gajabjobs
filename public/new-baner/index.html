NTA UGC NET December 2025 - Exam City
The UGC NET December 2025 Notification has been released by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for determining the eligibility of candidates for Assistant Professor and Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) in Indian universities and colleges. Candidates with a Master’s degree (55% marks for General and 50% for reserved categories) can apply online from 07 October to 07 November 2025 at ugcnet.nta.nic.in. SSC GD Constable Online Form 2026
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.

IOCL Non-Executive Personnel Online Form 2025
IOCL Non-Executive Recruitment 2025 has been announced by Indian Oil Corporation Limited for various non-executive posts in its Refineries Division. The recruitment is open for Diploma and B.Sc qualified candidates to fill Junior Engineering Assistant, Junior Technical Assistant and Quality Control Analyst positions across multiple refinery locations in India. The selection will be based on a Computer Based Test followed by a qualifying Skill/Proficiency/Physical Test. Online applications are invited from 20 December 2025 to 09 January 2026 with a pay scale ranging from ₹25,000 to ₹1,05,000 along with standard PSU allowances.

BSF Constable (GD) Sports Quota Online Form 2025
BSF Constable (GD) Sports Quota Recruitment 2025 has been announced for 549 posts for eligible male and female sportspersons across India. Candidates who are 10th pass and have valid sports achievements in recognized national or international competitions can apply online from 27/12/2025 to 15/01/2026 at rectt.bsf.gov.in. Selection will be done on the basis of sports merit, Physical Standard Test (PST), document verification and medical examination as per BSF recruitment rules.
