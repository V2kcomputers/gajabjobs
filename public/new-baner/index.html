MPPSC SET Exam 2025
The MP State Eligibility Test (MP SET 2025) is a state-level examination conducted by the Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission for determining eligibility for Assistant Professor recruitment in colleges and universities of Madhya Pradesh. The online application process will begin on 25 October 2025 and close on 20 November 2025. The written examination is scheduled to be held on 11 January 2026. Candidates are advised to review eligibility conditions, syllabus and subject list as per the official notification before submitting the application form.

CCRH Research Officer & Other Posts Answer Key 2025
The Central Council for Research in Homoeopathy (CCRH), under the Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India, has released the CCRH Recruitment 2025 Notification (Advt. No. 179/2025-26) for 48 vacancies across Group A, B and C posts. Eligible candidates can apply online from 05 November 2025 to 26 November 2025 through the official websites - ccrhindia.ayush.gov.in, ccrhonline.in or eapplynow.com. The recruitment includes posts like Research Officer, Junior Librarian, Pharmacist, X-Ray Technician, LDC, and Driver. Selection will be based on a Computer-Based Test (CBT) and an interview (for Group-A). Interested applicants should read the full notification for eligibility, qualification, age limit, and application fee details before applying.

CCRH Staff Nurse, MLT, Stenographer & ARO Answer Key 2025
Central Council for Research in Homoeopathy (CCRH) has released the official notification Advt. No. 180/2025-26 for 42 Group B & C vacancies including Staff Nurse, Medical Laboratory Technologist, Junior Stenographer, Junior MLT, and Assistant Research Officer (Pharmacognosy). The online application process will be open from 05/11/2025 to 26/11/2025. Candidates must apply through the official website www.ccrhindia.nic.in. The application fee is ₹500 for UR/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST/PwD/Female candidates are exempted. Selection will be based on a Computer Based Test (CBT) followed by document verification.

SSC Jr Engineer Answer Key 2025
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for the Junior Engineer (JE) Recruitment 2025. The vacancies are available in various departments such as BRO, CPWD, CWC, MES, NTRO, and others for trades including Civil, Electrical, and Mechanical. Candidates must have a Diploma or Degree in the relevant engineering discipline. Some departments also require two years of work experience. The application process is completely online, and the selection will be based on a Computer-Based Test (Paper 1), Paper 2, and Document Verification. This recruitment is open to both male and female candidates across India. Interested applicants can visit the official SSC website https://ssc.gov.in for more details and to apply online.
