Rajasthan State Eligibility Test (SET) 2026
The Rajasthan State Eligibility Test (SET) 2026 is being conducted by the University of Kota to determine the eligibility of candidates for the post of Assistant Professor in Rajasthan. Online applications will be accepted from 14 June 2026 to 15 July 2026, and the examination is scheduled to be held on 06 September 2026. Candidates must possess a Master's Degree with at least 55% marks (50% for reserved categories) from a recognized university. The examination will consist of two papers carrying a total of 150 questions and 300 marks, with no negative marking. The Rajasthan SET certificate will be valid for a lifetime and can be used for Assistant Professor recruitment within the state of Rajasthan.

Central Bank of India Apprentice Online Form 2026
Central Bank of India has invited online applications for 4500 Apprentice vacancies under the Apprentices Act, 1961.
