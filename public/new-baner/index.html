UP B.Ed. Admission 2026
UP B.Ed. Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) 2026 is conducted by Bundelkhand University, Jhansi for admission to the 2-year Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) course (2026–28). Candidates from General and OBC categories must have at least 50% marks in Graduation or Post-Graduation, while Engineering candidates require 55% marks. There is no minimum percentage requirement for SC/ST candidates. The selection process is based on the entrance examination followed by counselling.

CUET (UG) 2026 Online Correction Form
CUET (UG) 2026 is an all-India entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency for admission to undergraduate programs in Central and other participating universities for the 2026–27 session. Students who have passed or are appearing in Class 12 are eligible to apply. The test is conducted in computer-based mode, permits selection of up to five subjects, and follows a uniform marking scheme. Final admission is offered by universities based on CUET scores, cut-off lists, and course-specific eligibility requirements.

MPPSC SET Exam 2025
The MP State Eligibility Test (MP SET 2025) is a state-level examination conducted by the Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission for determining eligibility for Assistant Professor recruitment in colleges and universities of Madhya Pradesh. The online application process will begin on 25 October 2025 and close on 20 November 2025. The written examination is scheduled to be held on 11 January 2026. Candidates are advised to review eligibility conditions, syllabus and subject list as per the official notification before submitting the application form.
