MPESB ADDET Admission Form 2026
MP Employee Selection Board (MPESB) has released the notification for the Animal Husbandry and Dairy Technology Diploma Entrance Test (ADDET) 2026. Through this entrance examination, eligible candidates can secure admission to Animal Husbandry Diploma and Dairy Technology Diploma courses offered by government and private colleges in Madhya Pradesh. A total of 1100 seats are available across 11 colleges. Candidates who have passed Class 12 with Biology, Mathematics, or Agriculture subjects and are at least 17 years old as of 31 December 2026 can apply. The online application process begins on 05 June 2026 and closes on 19 June 2026, while the examination will start from 10 July 2026. Selection will be based on the entrance test merit and subsequent counselling process.

Bank of Baroda Apprentice Online Form 2026
Bank of Baroda has released the Apprentice Recruitment 2026 notification for 5000 posts across India under the Apprentices Act, 1961. Candidates having a Graduation degree in any discipline can apply online from 19/05/2026 to 08/06/2026. The age limit is 20 to 28 years as on 01/05/2026 with relaxation as per rules. Selected candidates will receive a stipend of ₹15,000 per month for one year training. The selection process includes Online Examination, Document Verification and Local Language Test. Candidates must register on NAPS/NATS portal before filling the online application form.

UPSC NDA II Online Form 2026
Union Public Service Commission has released the UPSC NDA II Recruitment 2026 notification for 394 posts.
