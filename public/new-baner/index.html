NTA UGC NET December 2025 - Exam City
The UGC NET December 2025 Notification has been released by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for determining the eligibility of candidates for Assistant Professor and Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) in Indian universities and colleges. RRB Group D Online Form 2026
RRB Level-1 / Group D Recruitment 2025 has been announced through CEN No. 09/2025 by the Railway Recruitment Boards for approximately 22000 vacancies across all RRB zones. The recruitment is for Level-1 posts under the 7th CPC Pay Matrix with an initial salary of ₹18000 per month. The online application process will be conducted in 2026 from 21 January to 20 February 2026 through the official RRB website. Candidates aged between 18 and 33 years as on 01 January 2026 will be eligible to apply, subject to relaxation as per rules. Detailed post-wise eligibility qualification selection process and syllabus will be released in the full notification on official RRB websites.

SSC GD Constable Online Form 2026
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.
