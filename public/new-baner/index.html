BRLPS JEEViKA Admit Card 2025
Bihar Rural Livelihoods Promotion Society (BRLPS), under the Rural Development Department of the Government of Bihar, has released the official notification for JEEViKA Recruitment 2025. A total of 2747 vacancies have been announced across various posts including Block Project Manager, Livelihood Specialist, Area Coordinator, Accountant, Office Assistant, Community Coordinator, and Block IT Executive. The required educational qualifications vary by post, ranging from Intermediate (for female Community Coordinators) to Graduation, Post-Graduation, Diploma, or B.Tech. The minimum age for applicants is 18 years, while the upper age limit goes up to 42 years depending on the category. The selection process involves a Computer-Based Test (CBT) and a typing test for relevant positions. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official website https://brlps.in from 30th July to 18th August 2025. This is a great opportunity to contribute to Bihar’s largest rural development mission and support the empowerment of rural communities.

UPPSC Computer Assistant Exam Date 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has released a notification for the recruitment of Computer Assistant posts in 2025. A total of 13 vacancies have been announced for eligible male and female candidates across India. To apply, candidates must have passed 10+2 (Intermediate) along with either a valid “O” Level certificate from NIELIT or a Diploma in Computer Science. The minimum age required is 18 years, and selected candidates will receive an initial salary in the ₹25,500 – ₹81,100 pay scale (Level-4). The application process is online only and will be available soon on the official website uppsc.up.nic.in. Interested candidates are advised to regularly check the website for the release of the online form and notification.

RSSB Jamadar Grade-II Exam Date 2025
Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSMSSB) has released the official notification for Jamadar Grade-II Recruitment 2025 under the Public Works Department (PWD). A total of 72 posts are available — 64 for Non-Scheduled Area and 8 for Scheduled Area. Eligible candidates who have passed Senior Secondary (12th) and possess a valid computer qualification (like RSCIT, DOEACC “O” Level, COPA, or Diploma in Computer Science) can apply online. The online application process will be active from 17/10/2025 to 15/11/2025, and the OMR-based exam will be conducted on 27/12/2025.
