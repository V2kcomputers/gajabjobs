IPPB Executive Result 2025
India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) has released the Executive Recruitment 2025 notification for 348 vacancies on engagement from existing Gramin Dak Sevaks (GDS) of the Department of Posts. Eligible GDS employees having a Graduate degree in any discipline can apply online from 09/10/2025 to 29/10/2025 at www.ippbonline.com. Selected candidates will receive a monthly salary of ₹30,000, and selection will be based on merit of graduation marks. The recruitment aims to strengthen IPPB’s doorstep banking network across India by engaging trained GDS executives.

BPSC ATP Assistant Town Planner Exam Date 2025
The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has announced 35 vacancies for Assistant Town Planner (ATP) under Advertisement No. 89/2025 in the Urban Development & Housing Department. The online application process will be open from 28th August 2025 to 22nd September 2025 on the official BPSC website. Candidates with a Post Graduate degree in Town Planning, Regional/Urban Planning, City Planning, Country/Transport Planning, Housing, or Environmental Planning are eligible to apply. The selection will be based on a written examination and interview, and appointed candidates will receive a salary under Pay Level 09 (₹53,100 – ₹1,67,800) along with allowances.

MPPSC Food Safety Officer Admit Card 2025
Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC) has released a notification for the recruitment of Food Safety Officer (FSO) with a total of 67 vacancies. The application process will be conducted online, and the job location is Madhya Pradesh. Candidates from all over India are eligible to apply, but those from other states will be treated as General (UR) category and will not receive any reservation or fee relaxation. Applicants must hold a Bachelor's, Master's, or Ph.D. degree in Food Technology, Agriculture, Biotechnology, Chemistry, Veterinary Science, Medicine, or a related field, or possess an equivalent qualification recognized by the Central Government. The age limit is 21 to 40 years, with age relaxation applicable as per rules for reserved categories. For complete details and to apply, visit the official website mppsc.mp.gov.in.

UP Police Assistant Operator Online Form 2025
UP Police has released the Assistant Operator Recruitment 2025 for 44 posts under the Police Radio Cadre. Eligible 12th PCM passed candidates aged 18–22 years can apply online from 03/12/2025 to 02/01/2026. The selection includes a written exam, PST, PET and document verification. Application fee is ₹500 for General/OBC/EWS and ₹400 for SC/ST.
