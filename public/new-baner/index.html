UPSC IES / ISS Admit Card 2026
Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released the notification for Indian Economic Service (IES) and Indian Statistical Service (ISS) Examination 2026. A total of 44 vacancies (16 for IES and 28 for ISS) are available. Eligible candidates can apply online from 11/02/2026 to 03/03/2026. The written examination will commence from 19/06/2026. Candidates must meet the prescribed educational qualifications in Economics or Statistics as per service requirement. The selection process includes a written examination followed by an interview.

Central Bank of India Apprentice Online Form 2026
Central Bank of India has invited online applications for 4500 Apprentice vacancies under the Apprentices Act, 1961. Eligible graduates who completed their degree on or after 01 January 2022 can apply from 12 June 2026 to 22 June 2026. The apprenticeship period is 12 months with a monthly stipend of ₹15,000. Selection will be based on an Online Examination, Local Language Test, Document Verification, and Medical Fitness. Candidates seeking banking sector experience should not miss this opportunity.
