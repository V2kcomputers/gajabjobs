RBI Assistant Online Form 2026
Reserve Bank of India has invited online applications for 650 Assistant posts under RBI Assistant Recruitment 2026. Candidates holding a Bachelor’s degree with minimum 50% marks (pass class for SC/ST/PwBD) and basic computer knowledge can apply from 16/02/2026 to 08/03/2026. The recruitment process consists of Preliminary Exam, Main Exam and Language Proficiency Test. The Prelims will be held on 11/04/2026 and the Mains on 07/06/2026. Selected candidates will receive a starting basic pay of ₹29,000 and an initial gross salary of around ₹58,514 per month.

UP Police Sub Inspector SI Admit Card 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has announced the UP Police Sub Inspector (SI) Recruitment 2025. The online application process will be open from 12 August 2025 to 11 September 2025, and fee adjustments for submitted payments can be made until 13 September 2025. The application fee is ₹500/- for General, EWS, and OBC candidates, while SC and ST candidates will have to pay ₹400/-. The recruitment is for posts in Uttar Pradesh, and applications must be submitted online.
