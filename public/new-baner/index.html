SBI Deputy Manager & AVP Vacancy 2026
The State Bank of India (SBI) has released the SBI SCO Recruitment 2026 notification for 116 Specialist Cadre Officer posts, including Assistant Vice President (IS Audit) and Deputy Manager (IS Audit and CA–Internal Audit). The online application process started on 23/02/2026 and will remain open until 15/03/2026. The selection will be based on shortlisting and interview only. The AVP post is contractual with a CTC up to ₹50 lakh, while the Deputy Manager posts are regular in MMGS-II scale. Candidates must meet the prescribed educational qualifications, relevant experience, and age criteria (21 to 45 years, depending on the post). The application fee is ₹750 for General/EWS/OBC candidates, while SC/ST/PwBD candidates are exempted from paying the fee.

CUET (UG) 2026 Online Form - Re-Open
CUET (UG) 2026 is an all-India entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency for admission to undergraduate programs in Central and other participating universities for the 2026–27 session. Students who have passed or are appearing in Class 12 are eligible to apply. The test is conducted in computer-based mode, permits selection of up to five subjects, and follows a uniform marking scheme. Final admission is offered by universities based on CUET scores, cut-off lists, and course-specific eligibility requirements.
