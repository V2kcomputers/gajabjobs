Union Bank of India Apprentice Vacancy 2026
Union Bank of India Apprentice Recruitment 2026 has been released for 1865 posts. The online application process started on 29 April 2026 and the last date to apply is 19 May 2026. RRB NTPC Inter Level Admit Card 2026
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released the notification for RRB NTPC Inter Level Recruitment 2025 (CEN 07/2025) with 3050 vacancies under the 10+2 (Undergraduate) category. Eligible candidates who have passed Intermediate (Class 12th) can apply online for posts like Junior Clerk cum Typist, Accounts Clerk cum Typist, Train Clerk, and Commercial cum Ticket Clerk. The application process will start from 28 October 2025 and continue till 27 November 2025. The selection process includes CBT-1, CBT-2, Typing Skill Test (for Clerk/Typist posts), Document Verification, and Medical Examination. The pay scale ranges from ₹19,900 to ₹21,700 (Level-2 & Level-3 as per 7th CPC) with additional allowances. Candidates are advised to check the official notification for complete eligibility, age limit, application fees, and exam details.
