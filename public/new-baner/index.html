Indian Navy SSR Medical 02/2027 Batch Vacancy 2026
Indian Navy has invited online applications from unmarried male candidates for SSR (Medical) 02/2027 batch. This recruitment is for Sailor posts in the Medical Branch for candidates who have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. The selection process includes the INET online exam, Physical Fitness Test, written examination, and medical examination. Selected candidates will receive training followed by an attractive salary, allowances, medical benefits, and a secure government career with an initial engagement of 20 years.

Shramodaya Admission Test Result 2026–27
Shramodaya Admission Test 2026–27 is an entrance examination conducted in Madhya Pradesh for admission to Shramodaya Awasiya Vidyalaya for Class 6, 7, 8 and 9. The admission is exclusively for children of registered construction workers under the MP Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board. The online application window will remain open from 24/12/2025 to 20/01/2026. Candidates will be selected through a written entrance test and final admission will be done on the basis of merit and seat availability.

Excellence School (SOE) & Model School (SOM) Class 9 Admission 2026–27 - Result
MP Excellence School Entrance Exam 2027 is a joint selection test for admission to Class 9 in Excellence Schools (SOE) and Model Schools (SOM) across Madhya Pradesh. Admission will be based on the entrance examination, with reservation benefits applicable as per state government norms. The application fee for all categories is ₹200 including portal charges.
