SBI Probationary Officer PO Vacancy 2026
State Bank of India (SBI) has released the SBI PO Recruitment 2026 notification for 1500 Probationary Officer (PO) vacancies. Eligible graduates can submit their online applications from 18 June 2026 to 08 July 2026. Candidates must be between 21 and 30 years of age as on 01 April 2026, with applicable age relaxation for reserved categories. The selection process includes a Preliminary Examination, Main Examination, Psychometric Test, Group Exercise, and Interview. Selected candidates will receive an attractive salary package with an approximate annual CTC of ₹21.97 lakh along with various allowances and benefits. Interested candidates should carefully read the official notification and complete the application process before the last date.

UPSC CAPF Assistant Commandant Exam Date 2026
UPSC has released the CAPF Assistant Commandant 2026 notification for 349 posts across BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP and SSB. The application window is open from 20/02/2026 to 12/03/2026 (6:00 PM). The written exam will be held on 19/07/2026. Applicants must possess a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university. The age limit is 20–25 years as on 01 August 2026, with relaxation for reserved categories as per rules. The recruitment process consists of Written Examination, PST, PET, Interview/Personality Test and Medical Examination. Both male and female candidates can apply online.
