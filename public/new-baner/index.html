RSSB REET Mains Upper Primary Teacher Level-II Online Form 2025- Exam Date
Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSMSSB) has released the REET Mains Level-II Upper Primary School Teacher Recruitment 2025 for a total of 2123 posts under the Sanskrit Education Department. Eligible candidates can apply online from 07/11/2025 to 06/12/2025 through the official website rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in. This recruitment includes vacancies for subjects like Sanskrit, Hindi, English, Social Science, and Maths-Science. Applicants must have a Graduation degree with B.Ed/D.El.Ed or 4-Year Integrated B.A./B.Sc. B.Ed as per NCTE norms. The application fee ranges from ₹400 to ₹600, and the exam is expected between 17/01/2026 to 21/01/2026. Interested candidates should check the official notification for eligibility, syllabus, and document requirements before applying.

RSSB REET Mains Primary Teacher Level-1 Online Form 2025 - Exam Date
Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSMSSB) has released the Rajasthan Primary Teacher Recruitment 2025 notification for a total of 5636 Level-1 posts under the Primary Education Department and Sanskrit Education Department. Eligible candidates who have passed REET Level-1 can apply online from 07/11/2025 to 06/12/2025 through the official website rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in. Shramodaya Admission Test Online Form 2026–27
Shramodaya Admission Test 2026–27 is an entrance examination conducted in Madhya Pradesh for admission to Shramodaya Awasiya Vidyalaya for Class 6, 7, 8 and 9. The admission is exclusively for children of registered construction workers under the MP Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board. The online application window will remain open from 24/12/2025 to 20/01/2026. Candidates will be selected through a written entrance test and final admission will be done on the basis of merit and seat availability.

RPSC Exam Calendar 2026
RPSC Exam Calendar 2026 has been officially released by the Rajasthan Public Service Commission, Ajmer. The calendar provides the tentative schedule of various recruitment examinations to be conducted in 2026, including Deputy Commandant, Lecturer, Sub Inspector, Veterinary Officer, Assistant Engineer, Senior Teacher, Junior Legal Officer, Statistical Officer and other posts. The exams will be held between January 2026 and December 2026, with some examinations conducted in CBRT (Computer Based Recruitment Test) mode. Detailed exam programmes, admit cards and shift information will be issued separately by RPSC as per schedule.
