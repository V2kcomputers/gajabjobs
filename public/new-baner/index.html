MP RTE Admission 2026-27 Allotment Letter Out
RTE Admission 2026-27 in Madhya Pradesh is conducted under the 25% reservation quota in private schools as per the Right to Education Act 2009. Children seeking admission in Nursery, KG-1 and Class 1 can apply through the official RTE portal. The process includes online registration, document verification, lottery-based school allotment and final admission confirmation. Applicants must meet age criteria, category eligibility (SC/ST/OBC/BPL/Divyang/HIV/COVID orphan/Weaker Section) and MP residence requirements.
