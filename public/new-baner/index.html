Indian Navy Agniveer Apprentice INET Online Form 2026
Indian Navy has invited online applications for Agniveer (Apprentice) 01/2027 and 02/2027 batches. Unmarried male candidates who have passed Class 10th and hold a 3-year AICTE-recognized Diploma with minimum 50% marks are eligible to apply. The recruitment process includes INET 2026, Physical Fitness Test, Written Examination, and Medical Examination. Selected candidates will receive attractive pay, allowances, insurance coverage, and Seva Nidhi benefits under the Agnipath Scheme. Eligible applicants can submit their online applications from 05 June 2026 to 29 June 2026.

UPSSSC Auditor & Assistant Accountant Online Form 2026
The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has invited online applications for the Auditor and Assistant Accountant Main Examination 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill **1829 vacancies** across various government departments in Uttar Pradesh. Candidates who have qualified **UPSSSC PET 2025** and meet the prescribed educational qualifications are eligible to apply. The online application process will commence on **14/07/2026** and remain open until **03/08/2026**.
