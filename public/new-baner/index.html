SBI Probationary Officers PO Final Result 2025 Out
The State Bank of India (SBI) has released the notification for the recruitment of 541 Probationary Officer (PO) posts, including 500 regular posts and 41 backlog posts. Candidates must have a Bachelor’s degree in any stream from a recognized university in India to be eligible. The category-wise distribution of vacancies is as follows: UR – 203, OBC – 135, EWS – 50, SC – 80, and ST – 73. The application process will be conducted online through the official SBI website at sbi.co.in. This is a golden opportunity for graduates seeking a prestigious career in banking with SBI.

DSSSB OTR One-Time Registration 2026
DSSSB One-Time Registration (OARS) allows candidates to create a unique login to apply for multiple posts under the Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board. MPESB Candidate Profiling / Profile Panjiyan Form Online
To apply for any government recruitment under the Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB), all candidates must complete the Candidate Profile Registration process. This is a one-time mandatory step where applicants register using Aadhaar eKYC, upload personal and academic details, and receive a unique Profile ID. This ID is required for all future MPESB applications. The process is simple, online, and essential for anyone seeking government jobs in Madhya Pradesh.
