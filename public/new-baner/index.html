IBPS Deputy Manager Online Form 2026
IBPS has announced the Deputy Manager – AI Application Developer (Grade E) Recruitment 2026 for regular appointment in Mumbai. The online application window is open from 25/02/2026 to 10/03/2026. Applicants must be 23 to 30 years old as on 01/02/2026 and possess B.E./B.Tech in Computer Science, AI, Data Science, or Machine Learning, or MCA/M.Sc (Computer Science) with at least three years of post-qualification experience in AI/ML, Generative AI, Large Language Models (LLMs), NLP, Python programming, and database management systems. The selection process comprises an Online Examination, Skill Test, Group Exercise, and Personal Interview. The offered annual CTC is approximately ₹17.15 lakh, and selected candidates must sign a ₹2 lakh service bond for a minimum of three years.
