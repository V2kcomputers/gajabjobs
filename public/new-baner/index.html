UP RTE Admission 2026
Under RTE Admission 2026, children from disadvantaged and weaker sections will get free admission on 25% seats in entry-level classes (Nursery, LKG, UKG, Class 1) in recognized private schools. The application process is fully online, and parents can select up to 10 neighbourhood schools. Selection will be done through a transparent lottery system. Age criteria, eligibility conditions, document verification, BSA locking process, and admission orders will follow the official schedule. The government will reimburse school fees and also provide financial support for books and uniforms.

Excellence & Model School Class 9 Admission 2026–27 - Admit Card
MP Excellence School Entrance Exam 2027 is a joint selection test for admission to Class 9 in Excellence Schools (SOE) and Model Schools (SOM) across Madhya Pradesh. MP resident students who have passed or are appearing in Class 8 can submit the online application between 30/12/2025 and 16/01/2026. Admission will be based on the entrance examination, with reservation benefits applicable as per state government norms. The application fee for all categories is ₹200 including portal charges.
