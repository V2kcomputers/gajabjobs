MPPSC SET Exam 2025
The MP State Eligibility Test (MP SET 2025) is a state-level examination conducted by the Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission for determining eligibility for Assistant Professor recruitment in colleges and universities of Madhya Pradesh. Candidates are advised to review eligibility conditions, syllabus and subject list as per the official notification before submitting the application form.
