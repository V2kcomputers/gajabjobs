Central Bank of India Apprentice Online Form 2026
Central Bank of India has invited online applications for 4500 Apprentice vacancies under the Apprentices Act, 1961. Eligible graduates who completed their degree on or after 01 January 2022 can apply from 12 June 2026 to 22 June 2026. The apprenticeship period is 12 months with a monthly stipend of ₹15,000. RRB Section Controller Online Form 2026
The Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) have issued the RRB Section Controller Recruitment 2026 short notification under CEN No. 03/2026. A total of 119 vacancies are available for the Section Controller post. Candidates with a Bachelor's Degree from a recognized university can apply for this recruitment. The prescribed age limit is 20 to 33 years as of 01 July 2026. Selected candidates will be appointed under Pay Level-6 and receive an initial salary of ₹35,400 per month. The online application process is tentatively scheduled from 15 July 2026 to 14 August 2026. Applicants are advised to review the detailed notification for complete information regarding eligibility, age relaxation, application fee, and recruitment procedure.

CSIR Assistant Section Officer ASO Online Form 2026
Council of Scientific & Industrial Research (CSIR) invites online applications for 100 Assistant Section Officer (ASO) posts in General, Finance & Accounts, and Stores & Purchase cadres. Candidates with a Bachelor's Degree who are non-recommended candidates of UPSC Civil Services Examination (CSE) 2024 are eligible to apply. The online application process will remain open from 23/06/2026 to 13/07/2026. The maximum age limit is 33 years with applicable category-wise relaxation. UR/OBC/EWS candidates are required to pay an application fee of ₹500, while SC/ST/PwBD/Women/Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted. Selection will be based on UPSC CSE 2024 marks followed by a qualifying Computer Proficiency Test (CPT).
