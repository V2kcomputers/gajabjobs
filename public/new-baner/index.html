SSC GD Constable Online Form 2026
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.

Uttar Pradesh Home Guard Online Correction Form 2025
The Uttar Pradesh Home Guard Recruitment 2025 has announced 41,424 vacancies for Home Guard (Male/Female) posts across all districts of the state. The online application process will run from 18 November 2025 to 17 December 2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10 (High School) from a recognized board and must be between 18 to 30 years of age as on 01 July 2025. The selection process includes Physical Standards Test (PST) and Physical Efficiency Test (PET), where male candidates must complete 4.8 km in 28 minutes and female candidates must complete 2.4 km in 16 minutes. The application fee is ₹400 for General/OBC/EWS and ₹300 for SC/ST candidates. The complete district-wise vacancy list and PET/PST rules are provided in the official notification.

MPESB MP Police Constable Answer Key 2025
The Madhya Pradesh Professional Examination Board (MPESB) has announced the MP Police Constable Recruitment 2025 for a total of 7500 Constable (General Duty – SAF & DEF) posts. The online application process will start from 15/09/2025 and remain open till 29/09/2025, while the correction window will be available up to 04/10/2025. The written examination is scheduled to begin from 30/10/2025. Candidates applying for this recruitment must have passed 10th/12th (for UR/SC/OBC/EWS) or at least 8th standard (for ST candidates) from a recognized board. The minimum age limit is 18 years and the maximum is 33 years for general category male candidates, with age relaxations applicable for reserved categories and women as per rules. The application fee is ₹500 for UR, ₹250 for SC/ST/OBC/EWS (MP domicile), while departmental candidates need to pay ₹200 (UR) or ₹100 (reserved categories) along with applicable portal charges. This recruitment offers a great opportunity for candidates seeking a stable government job in the Madhya Pradesh Police Department.

IB Security Assistant/Executive Result 2025
The Intelligence Bureau (IB), under the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), has released a notification for the recruitment of Security Assistant/Executive posts for the year 2025. A total of 4987 vacancies are available across various states and Union Territories. Candidates must have passed 10th standard, possess knowledge of the local language/dialect, and hold a domicile certificate of the region applied for. The post offers a Level-3 pay scale (₹21,700 – ₹69,100) along with special allowances. This is a central government Group 'C' non-gazetted, non-ministerial post, offering job security and growth in the intelligence sector. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official portal before the last date.
