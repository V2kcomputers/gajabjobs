CCRH Research Officer & Other Posts Exam City 2025
The Central Council for Research in Homoeopathy (CCRH), under the Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India, has released the CCRH Recruitment 2025 Notification (Advt. No. 179/2025-26) for 48 vacancies across Group A, B and C posts. Eligible candidates can apply online from 05 November 2025 to 26 November 2025 through the official websites - ccrhindia.ayush.gov.in, ccrhonline.in or eapplynow.com. The recruitment includes posts like Research Officer, Junior Librarian, Pharmacist, X-Ray Technician, LDC, and Driver. Selection will be based on a Computer-Based Test (CBT) and an interview (for Group-A). Interested applicants should read the full notification for eligibility, qualification, age limit, and application fee details before applying.

CCRH Staff Nurse, MLT, Stenographer & ARO Exam City 2025
Central Council for Research in Homoeopathy (CCRH) has released the official notification Advt. No. 180/2025-26 for 42 Group B & C vacancies including Staff Nurse, Medical Laboratory Technologist, Junior Stenographer, Junior MLT, and Assistant Research Officer (Pharmacognosy). The online application process will be open from 05/11/2025 to 26/11/2025. Candidates must apply through the official website www.ccrhindia.nic.in. The application fee is ₹500 for UR/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST/PwD/Female candidates are exempted. Selection will be based on a Computer Based Test (CBT) followed by document verification.

IB Multi Tasking Staff Online Form 2025
The Intelligence Bureau has released the IB MTS Recruitment 2025 notification for 362 Multi-Tasking Staff (General) posts across various SIBs in India. Candidates who have passed Class 10 and possess a valid state domicile certificate can apply online from 22/11/2025 to 14/12/2025. The selection process includes a Tier-I objective exam and a Tier-II descriptive exam. Application fees are ₹650 for General/OBC/EWS male applicants and ₹550 for all other categories.

KVS NVS Teaching & Non-Teaching Online Form 2025
KVS NVS Recruitment 2025 offers more than 14967 Teaching and Non-Teaching vacancies under Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan and Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti. Eligible candidates can apply online from 14/11/2025 to 04/12/2025 for posts such as PRT, TGT, PGT, Principal, Clerk, Stenographer, Librarian and others. The application fee is ₹1500 for General, OBC and EWS categories, while SC, ST, PwD and all female candidates are exempted from fees. Qualifications vary by post and include 10th, 12th, Graduation, Post Graduation, B.Ed, D.El.Ed and CTET. The selection process includes a computer-based test followed by document verification.

SSC GD Constable Online Form 2026
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.
