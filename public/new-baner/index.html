MP RTE Admission 2026-27 Online Form
RTE Admission 2026-27 in Madhya Pradesh is conducted under the 25% reservation quota in private schools as per the Right to Education Act 2009. Children seeking admission in Nursery, KG-1 and Class 1 can apply through the official RTE portal.
