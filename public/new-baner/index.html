UP Police Constable Online Correction Form 2025
UP Police Constable Recruitment 2025 has been announced for a total of 32,679 posts including Constable Civil Police, PAC, Special Security Force, Female Battalion, Mounted Police and Jail Warder posts. Candidates who have passed 12th (Intermediate) are eligible to apply. The age limit is 18–22 years for male and 18–25 years for female candidates, with relaxation for reserved categories as per rules. The selection process includes a written examination, document verification, Physical Standard Test (PST), Physical Efficiency Test (PET) and medical examination. The online application process will start from 31/12/2025 and the last date to apply is 30/01/2026. This recruitment offers a significant opportunity for candidates seeking a government job in Uttar Pradesh Police.

CUET (UG) 2026 Online Form
CUET (UG) 2026 is an all-India entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency for admission to undergraduate programs in Central and other participating universities for the 2026–27 session. Students who have passed or are appearing in Class 12 are eligible to apply. The test is conducted in computer-based mode, permits selection of up to five subjects, and follows a uniform marking scheme. Final admission is offered by universities based on CUET scores, cut-off lists, and course-specific eligibility requirements.
