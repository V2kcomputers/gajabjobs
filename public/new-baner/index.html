Indian Army Agniveer Rally Recruitment 2026
Indian Army Rally Recruitment 2026-27 notification has been released for Agniveer GD, Technical, Assistant, Tradesman, Women Military Police, Soldier Technical (Nursing Assistant), Sepoy Pharma and JCO Religious Teacher posts. Online applications start from 13/02/2026 and the last date to apply is 01/04/2026. Candidates must fulfill post-wise educational qualification, age limit and physical eligibility criteria. UP RTE Admission 2026
Under RTE Admission 2026, children from disadvantaged and weaker sections will get free admission on 25% seats in entry-level classes (Nursery, LKG, UKG, Class 1) in recognized private schools. The application process is fully online, and parents can select up to 10 neighbourhood schools. Selection will be done through a transparent lottery system. Age criteria, eligibility conditions, document verification, BSA locking process, and admission orders will follow the official schedule. The government will reimburse school fees and also provide financial support for books and uniforms.

NTA NCET 2026 Online Form
NCET 2026 is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to the 4-Year Integrated Teacher Education Programme (ITEP). Candidates who have passed or are appearing in Class 12 can apply. The exam is held in CBT mode and covers Language, Teaching Aptitude, General Test and Domain subjects. Admission is granted through counselling based on exam performance.

Indian Army Men NCC 124th Course Oct 2026
Indian Army invites applications for NCC Special Entry Scheme 124th Course (Oct 2026) under Short Service Commission (Non-Technical) for Men. A total of 70 posts (63 General and 07 for Wards of Battle Casualties) are available. Eligible candidates must be unmarried male graduates holding NCC ‘C’ Certificate with minimum ‘B’ Grade. The selection procedure includes shortlisting based on NCC grading and graduation percentage, followed by SSB interview, medical examination, and final merit list. Selected candidates will receive 49 weeks of pre-commission training at Officers Training Academy (OTA), Chennai. The online application process will remain open from 12/02/2026 to 16/03/2026.
