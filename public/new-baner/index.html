IB Security Assistant/Executive Result 2025
The Intelligence Bureau (IB), under the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), has released a notification for the recruitment of Security Assistant/Executive posts for the year 2025. A total of 4987 vacancies are available across various states and Union Territories. Candidates must have passed 10th standard, possess knowledge of the local language/dialect, and hold a domicile certificate of the region applied for. The post offers a Level-3 pay scale (₹21,700 – ₹69,100) along with special allowances. This is a central government Group 'C' non-gazetted, non-ministerial post, offering job security and growth in the intelligence sector. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official portal before the last date.

MPESB Group 1 Subgroup 2 Online Form 2025
MPESB Group 1 Subgroup 2 Recruitment 2025 has been announced for a total of 474 various posts under different departments of the Government of Madhya Pradesh. UP Police Computer Operator Grade-A Online Form 2025
Police Computer Operator Grade-A Recruitment 2025 has been announced by the Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) for a total of 1352 vacancies. The recruitment is for the post of Police Computer Operator Grade-A. Eligible candidates who have passed Intermediate (10+2) and possess a valid computer qualification certificate can apply online from 16/12/2025 to 15/01/2026 through the official UPPRPB website. Selection will be done through a written examination followed by document verification, as per the official notification.
