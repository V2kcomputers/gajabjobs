Indian Navy 10+2 B.Tech Cadet Entry July 2026 Online Form
Indian Navy 10+2 (B.Tech) Cadet Entry Recruitment 2026 is conducted for admission to a four-year B.Tech course at the Indian Naval Academy, Ezhimala, for grant of Permanent Commission in Executive and Technical Branches. Unmarried male and female candidates who have appeared in JEE (Main) 2025 are eligible to apply. Selection is based on JEE Main CRL shortlisting, SSB Interview, and medical fitness. The recruitment is for 44 posts, including a maximum of 07 posts for women. The online application window is open from 03 January 2026 to 19 January 2026, and no application fee is required.

CUET (UG) 2026 Online Form
CUET (UG) 2026 is an all-India entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency for admission to undergraduate programs in Central and other participating universities for the 2026–27 session. Students who have passed or are appearing in Class 12 are eligible to apply. The test is conducted in computer-based mode, permits selection of up to five subjects, and follows a uniform marking scheme. Final admission is offered by universities based on CUET scores, cut-off lists, and course-specific eligibility requirements.

RRB Group D Online Form 2026
RRB Level-1 / Group D Recruitment 2025 has been announced through CEN No. 09/2025 by the Railway Recruitment Boards for approximately 22000 vacancies across all RRB zones. The recruitment is for Level-1 posts under the 7th CPC Pay Matrix with an initial salary of ₹18000 per month. The online application process will be conducted in 2026 from 21 January to 20 February 2026 through the official RRB website. Candidates aged between 18 and 33 years as on 01 January 2026 will be eligible to apply, subject to relaxation as per rules. Detailed post-wise eligibility qualification selection process and syllabus will be released in the full notification on official RRB websites.
