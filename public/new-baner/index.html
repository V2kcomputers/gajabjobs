MPESB Group 1 Subgroup 2 Online Form 2025
MPESB Group 1 Subgroup 2 Recruitment 2025 has been announced for a total of 474 various posts under different departments of the Government of Madhya Pradesh. Candidates having a postgraduate degree in the relevant subject as per departmental recruitment rules are eligible to apply. The online application process will start from 24 December 2025 and will remain open till 07 January 2026 through the official website esb.mp.gov.in. The selection will be conducted through a written examination followed by further procedures as mentioned in the official notification.

UP Police Computer Operator Grade-A Online Form 2025
Police Computer Operator Grade-A Recruitment 2025 has been announced by the Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) for a total of 1352 vacancies. The recruitment is for the post of Police Computer Operator Grade-A. Eligible candidates who have passed Intermediate (10+2) and possess a valid computer qualification certificate can apply online from 16/12/2025 to 15/01/2026 through the official UPPRPB website. Selection will be done through a written examination followed by document verification, as per the official notification.
