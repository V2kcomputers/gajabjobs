UPSC NDA 1 2026 - Exam Date
UPSC NDA & NA (I) 2026 offers a total of 394 vacancies across Army, Navy and Air Force wings. Eligible unmarried male and female candidates born between 02 July 2007 and 01 July 2010 with 10+2 qualification can apply online until 30 December 2025. Selection includes a written exam, SSB interview and medical test, with the final merit determining admission to NDA and Naval Academy courses starting January 2027.

SSC GD Constable 2026 - Correction Start
The SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2026 has been announced for a total of 25,487 vacancies across various forces. The online application process will be conducted from 01/12/2025 to 31/12/2025. Candidates must have passed Class 10th (Matriculation) to be eligible. The selection process includes a Computer-Based Examination (CBT), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Medical Examination. The application fee is ₹100 for General/OBC/EWS, while SC/ST, Female, and Ex-Servicemen candidates are exempted from the fee. Detailed force-wise vacancies, age limit, physical standards, and exam pattern are provided in the official notification.

RSSB REET Mains Upper Primary Teacher Level-II Vacancy 2025 - Admit Card
Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSMSSB) has released the REET Mains Level-II Upper Primary School Teacher Recruitment 2025 for a total of 2123 posts under the Sanskrit Education Department. Eligible candidates can apply online from 07/11/2025 to 06/12/2025 through the official website rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in. This recruitment includes vacancies for subjects like Sanskrit, Hindi, English, Social Science, and Maths-Science. Applicants must have a Graduation degree with B.Ed/D.El.Ed or 4-Year Integrated B.A./B.Sc. B.Ed as per NCTE norms. The application fee ranges from ₹400 to ₹600, and the exam is expected between 17/01/2026 to 21/01/2026. Interested candidates should check the official notification for eligibility, syllabus, and document requirements before applying.

RSSB REET Mains Primary Teacher Level-1 Vacancy 2025- Admit Card
Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSMSSB) has released the Rajasthan Primary Teacher Recruitment 2025 notification for a total of 5636 Level-1 posts under the Primary Education Department and Sanskrit Education Department. Eligible candidates who have passed REET Level-1 can apply online from 07/11/2025 to 06/12/2025 through the official website rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in. The recruitment includes General and Sanskrit teachers for both Non-TSP and TSP areas. Selection will be based on merit and eligibility as per REET qualification.
