Hindustan Copper Limited (HCL) has released the Executive Recruitment 2026 notification for 70 vacancies in various disciplines including Mining, Electrical, Mechanical, Finance, HR, Geology, Civil, Medical, and other departments. NTA NEET UG 2026 Re Exam City Details
NEET (UG) 2026 is a national-level entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate medical courses across India. It is mandatory for admission to MBBS, BDS, AYUSH, BVSc & AH, and B.Sc Nursing (MNS) courses. Candidates must be 17 years old by 31 December 2026 and must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English. There is no upper age limit or attempt restriction. The examination will be conducted offline on 03 May 2026 for a duration of three hours in 13 languages.
