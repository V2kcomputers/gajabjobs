NTA JEE Mains Session II Online Form 2026
JEE (Main) 2026 Session 2 will be conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) between 02 April and 09 April 2026. The online application process for Session 2 will be open from 01 February 2026 to 25 February 2026 (up to 9:00 PM), and the last date for payment of the application fee is 25 February 2026 (up to 11:50 PM). Candidates who have already applied for JEE (Main) 2026 Session 1 and wish to appear in Session 2 must log in using their existing application number and password to select papers, exam cities, and pay the Session 2 fee, while new candidates must apply afresh. Details regarding city intimation, admit card download, and result declaration will be announced on the official JEE (Main) portal in due course, and candidates are strictly advised to submit only one application form, as multiple applications will be treated as unfair means.

NTA NEET UG Online Form 2026
NEET (UG) 2026 is a national-level entrance examination conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate medical courses across India. It is mandatory for admission to MBBS, BDS, AYUSH, BVSc & AH, and B.Sc Nursing (MNS) courses. Candidates must be 17 years old by 31 December 2026 and must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English. There is no upper age limit or attempt restriction. The examination will be conducted offline on 03 May 2026 for a duration of three hours in 13 languages.

Bihar RTE Admission 2026–27
RTE 25% Admission 2026–27 is offered under the Right to Education Act to ensure free education in private schools for children belonging to EWS, SC, ST, OBC, minority and orphan categories. Children aged 6 years or above as on 01 April 2026 who satisfy the income criteria can apply online through the Gyandeep Portal. Admission is done after document verification and online school allotment through a transparent random selection process.

RRB Section Controller Recruitment 2025- Admit Card
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has announced 368 vacancies for Section Controller posts through CEN 04/2025. The online application process will be open from 15 September to 14 October 2025.
