How To Apply for SSC One Time Registration (OTR) 2025 ‚Äì Complete Guide Vacancy :
  • Visit Official Website: Go to ssc.gov.in
  • Open OTR Registration: Click on One Time Registration / OTR link
  • Enter Personal Details: Fill name, date of birth, gender, category, and contact details
  • Enter Educational Details: Provide qualification, year of passing, and marks obtained
