How To Apply for RRB Exam Calendar Vacancy 2026 :
  • Visit Official Website: Open the official Railway Recruitment Board website of the concerned RRB.
  • New Registration: Click on the recruitment link and complete basic registration using name, mobile number and email ID.
  • Login: Use the registration number and password to log in to the application portal.
  • Fill Application Form: Enter personal details, educational qualification, category and other required information carefully.
  • Upload Documents: Upload scanned photograph, signature and other documents in the prescribed format and size.
  • Choose Exam Preferences: Select post, RRB zone, exam language and other preferences as asked.
  • Pay Application Fee: Pay the application fee online through debit card, credit card, net banking or UPI.
  • Final Submit: Review all details, submit the form and download/print the confirmation page for future reference.
