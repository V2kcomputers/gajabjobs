How To Apply for EMRS Teaching & Non-Teaching Vacancy 2025 :
  • Visit the official EMRS recruitment website: Go to the EMRS/NESTS portal to start your application.
  • Register as a new user: Enter your basic details like name, DOB, email, and mobile number.
  • Login using your credentials: Use your Registration ID and password to access the application form.
  • Fill in personal and educational details: Provide category, gender, nationality, and qualifications.
  • Upload photograph and signature: Follow the specified format and size guidelines.
  • Select the post(s) you want to apply for: Choose from Principal, PGT, TGT, and Non-Teaching posts.
  • Pay the application fee online: Use Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking, or UPI as applicable.
  • Preview all details carefully: Check for errors or missing information before submission.
  • Submit the application form: Complete the process by clicking the submit button.
  • Download and save the confirmation page: Keep it for future reference and verification.
