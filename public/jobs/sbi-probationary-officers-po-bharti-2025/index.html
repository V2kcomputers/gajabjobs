How To Apply for Probationary Officers Vacancy 2025 :
  • Visit the Official Website: Go to sbi.co.in and click on Careers section.
  • Click on Current Openings: Under Join SBI, select Current Openings and find SBI PO Recruitment 2025.
  • Open the Apply Link: Click on the Apply Online link next to the PO post.
  • Register Yourself: Click on New Registration. Fill in your basic details (name, email, mobile number). Submit and note down the Registration ID and Password.
  • Fill the Application Form: Log in using your credentials. Fill your personal, educational, and contact details correctly.
  • Upload Documents: Upload recent passport size photo and signature. Follow the prescribed format and size mentioned in the notification.
  • Preview the Application: Check all details carefully before final submission.
  • Pay the Application Fee: Pay the exam fee using Debit Card / Credit Card / Net Banking / UPI. Save the payment receipt.
  • Submit the Form: After successful payment, submit the final application form.
  • Print the Confirmation Page: Download and print the confirmation page for future reference.
