How To Apply for Civilian Posts Vacancy 2025 :
  • Visit the official website: Go to www.joinindiannavy.gov.in.
  • Read the latest notification: Carefully go through the INCET 01/2025 recruitment advertisement.
  • Go to the Candidate Online Form section: Click on the Apply Online or Candidate Login section.
  • Complete the registration: Fill in your basic details to generate a registration number and password.
  • Fill out the application form: Enter educational, personal, and communication details correctly.
  • Pay the application fee: Pay the examination fee through online mode only (via UPI/Net banking/Debit card).
  • Print the application form: Download and print a copy of the filled form for future reference.
