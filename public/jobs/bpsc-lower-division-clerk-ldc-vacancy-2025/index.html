How To Apply for Lower Division Clerk LDC Vacancy 2025 :
  • ✅ Visit the Official Website: Go to the official website of Bihar Public Service Commission www.bpsc.bih.nic.in
  • ✅ Read the Latest Notification: Click on the Lower Division Clerk 2025 recruitment notification and read it carefully for eligibility, important dates, and instructions.
  • ✅ Go to the Candidate Online Form Section: Click on the Apply Online link available on the homepage or under the Online Application section.
  • ✅ Complete the Registration: Register yourself by entering basic details like name, mobile number, email, and date of birth.
  • ✅ Fill Out the Application Form: Login using your credentials and fill out the application form with personal, educational, and other required details.
  • ✅ Upload Documents: Upload scanned copies of your photograph, signature, and supporting documents as per the given format and size.
  • ✅ Pay the Application Fee: Pay the prescribed application fee through online payment methods like debit/credit card, UPI, or net banking.
  • ✅ Print the Application Form: After successful submission, download and print the completed application form and fee receipt for future reference.
