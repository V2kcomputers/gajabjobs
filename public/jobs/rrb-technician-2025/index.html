How To Apply for Technician Vacancy 2025 :
  • Visit the official website: rrbapply.gov.in
  • Register New Account: Click on New Registration, enter name, mobile number, and email ID. Verify using OTP.
  • Login and Fill the Form: Enter personal, educational, and category details.
  • Upload Documents: Recent photo, signature, applicable certificates (SC/ST/PH).
  • Pay Application Fee: Pay via debit card, credit card, net banking or UPI.
  • Submit the Form: Review and click on Final Submit.
  • Print Application: Save/print the submitted form.
