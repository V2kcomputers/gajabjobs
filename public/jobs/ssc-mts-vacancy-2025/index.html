How To Apply for Multi Tasking Staff (MTS) – Non-Technical & Havaldar in CBIC and CBN Vacancy 2025 :
  • Visit the Official Website: Go to ssc.nic.in, the official website of the Staff Selection Commission.
  • Read the Latest Notification: Download and carefully read the SSC MTS 2025 official notification to check eligibility and important details.
  • Go to the Candidate Online Form Section: Click on the Apply tab and select MTS to access the online application link.
  • Complete the Registration: New users must register using a valid email ID and mobile number to generate login credentials.
  • Fill Out the Application Form: Log in using your credentials and fill in your personal, educational, and contact details.
  • Pay the Application Fee: Pay the required fee online through Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, or UPI.
  • Print the Application Form: After successful submission, download and print the filled application form for future reference.
